Conclusions From This Research



We have only looked at the impact living in a town or a village has on the students and ultimately wanted to see if any students were at an advantage due to this. From the plots we have produced there is nothing to suggest that either of these demographics are benefitting over the other.



However, this is not a well-balanced dataset as we have more than twice as many students from villages than towns. We would benefit from gathering some more data for students from towns to bring this closer to the total of students we have from villages. This should then give us a truer understanding of how the two are in comparison. Some further questions that I would also like to answer from this dataset are; how does family income affect the students performance; how does the family income relate to the school type the student attends and how are students from the different school types performing?
